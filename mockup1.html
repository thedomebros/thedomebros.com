How the install works

Measured, anchored, and dialed in for your pool.

Measure the pool & deck

We review pool shape, surrounding concrete, and blower placement so the dome is sized for your yard.

Install the anchors

The vinyl perimeter is fastened to anchors set into the deck — a secure base for the enclosure.

Inflate, seal & service

We inflate it, check the seal, tune the air pressure, and can take it down and reinstall each year.
