About TheDomeBros

Swimmers who didn't want summer to end

We're a family of brothers who never liked watching the pool sit unused for half the year — so we did something about it.

Our story

We never liked the idea that a backyard pool only gets used a few months a year. After we invested in a pool dome for our own backyard just so we could keep swimming through the cold months, it changed everything — the pool went from a short summer season to something we used all year long.

The surprising part was how few pool owners knew this was even an option for a pool they already had. We started TheDomeBros to change that: to help other owners get year-round use out of the pool they've already invested in, without building a permanent room around it.

Why we do this

A backyard pool is a big investment to install and maintain. An air-supported pool dome protects that investment and stretches it across every season — swim in the winter, stay out of the wind and rain, and keep the whole family in the water year-round.

We serve homeowners throughout Utah County — including Provo, Orem, Lehi, American Fork, Spanish Fork, Pleasant Grove, Saratoga Springs, Eagle Mountain, Highland, Alpine, Cedar Hills, Mapleton, Springville, and Payson.

A pool enclosure without the permanent build

An air-supported pool dome is a removable pool enclosure — it gives your backyard pool the feel of an indoor pool without framing a permanent glass or screen room around it.

Because the enclosure is held up by gentle air pressure instead of poles or walls, it goes up for the cold months and comes down when you want your open-air pool back. You get a true year-round pool enclosure that costs a fraction of a permanent structure and leaves no fixed building behind.
