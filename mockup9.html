Pool dome sales & installation

Your pool. In season, all year.

We sell and install air-supported vinyl pool domes that anchor into your concrete deck, hold their shape with gentle air pressure, and keep your pool usable as the weather changes.

Climate-controlled comfort

Year-round use

Value from your pool well beyond summer — family, training, therapy, and entertaining.

How the install works

02 — Process
01

Measure

We review pool shape, deck concrete, and blower placement so the dome is sized for your yard.

02

Anchor

The vinyl perimeter is fastened to anchors set into the deck for a secure base.

03

Inflate & service

We inflate, seal, tune the pressure, and can take it down and reinstall each year.
