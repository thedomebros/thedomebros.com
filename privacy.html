Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 18, 2026

This policy explains what information TheDomeBros collects through this website, why, and how it is handled.

Who we are

This website is operated by TheDomeBros LLC ("TheDomeBros," "we," "us"), serving Utah County. You can reach us about privacy at contact@thedomebros.com.

Information we collect

We only collect what you choose to send us through the quote request form: your name, email address, phone number, approximate pool size, your message, and any photos or files you optionally attach. We do not ask for payment information on this site.

When analytics is enabled, we also collect standard, aggregated usage data (such as pages viewed, approximate location, device and browser type) to understand how the site is used and how visitors arrive.

How we use it

Quote-form information is used solely to respond to your request, prepare an estimate, and communicate with you about pool dome sales and installation. Usage data is used to operate, measure, and improve the site and our advertising.

Service providers

We use a small number of third parties strictly to run this site and respond to you:

• Cloudflare — processes the quote form submission.
• Resend — delivers the quote email to us and the confirmation email to you.
• GitHub Pages — hosts the website.
• Google Analytics — measures site usage (when enabled).

These providers process data on our behalf to provide their service; we do not sell your personal information.

Cookies & analytics

The site itself does not require cookies to function. If analytics is enabled, Google Analytics may set cookies or similar identifiers to measure usage. You can block cookies in your browser, and most browsers offer a "Do Not Track" setting.

Data retention

We keep quote-request emails for as long as needed to respond to and follow up on your inquiry, and as required for our normal business records. You may ask us to delete your information at any time.

Your choices

You can request access to, correction of, or deletion of the personal information you submitted by emailing contact@thedomebros.com. Depending on where you live, you may have additional rights under laws such as the CCPA; contact us and we will honor applicable requests.

Children

This site is intended for adults arranging services for their property and is not directed to children under 13.

Changes

We may update this policy as the business or the site changes. The "last updated" date above reflects the current version.

Contact

Questions about this policy or your information: contact@thedomebros.com.
