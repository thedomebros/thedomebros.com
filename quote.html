Quick answers

The three things everyone asks first.

Will it damage my pool deck?

The dome anchors into your existing concrete deck with a row of small, low-profile anchors, installed cleanly with the right hardware. They stay in place so seasonal re-installs are quick — and when the dome is down for the summer, they sit flush with the deck, so you walk right over them without noticing. Nothing about the pool itself is changed.

What happens in snow?

Light snow sheds off the curved surface. Ahead of severe wind or heavy snow, you switch off the blower and the dome settles onto the water like a pool cover — then re-inflates in about half an hour once the storm passes.

How long does installation take?

Once your dome arrives, installation is typically completed in a single visit — we anchor, assemble, inflate, and walk you through operating it. We confirm scheduling along with your quote.
