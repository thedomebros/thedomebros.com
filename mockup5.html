How the install works

A straightforward, professional process.

STEP 01

Measure

We review pool shape, deck concrete, and blower placement so the dome fits your yard.

STEP 02

Anchor

The vinyl perimeter is fastened to anchors set into the deck for a secure base.

STEP 03

Inflate & service

We inflate, seal, tune the pressure, and can take it down and reinstall each year.
