What a dome offers

Pool season shouldn't end with the summer.

Comfort

The enclosure traps warmth and blocks wind for a more comfortable swim around your existing pool.

Year-round use

Value from your pool well beyond summer — for family, training, therapy, and entertaining.

Anchored

A secure anchor system set into the surrounding concrete keeps the structure positioned.

Air-supported

A quiet blower holds the dome with gentle pressure — no poles, no heavy framing.

The process

Measured, anchored, maintained.

Measure

We review pool shape, deck concrete, and blower placement so the dome suits your yard.

Anchor

The vinyl perimeter is fastened to anchors set into the deck for a secure base.

Service

We inflate, seal, tune the pressure, and can take it down and reinstall each year.
