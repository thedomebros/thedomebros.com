What a dome gives your backyard pool.

Climate-controlled comfort

The enclosure helps trap warmth, block wind, and create a more comfortable swim around your existing outdoor pool.

Year-round pool use

Extend the value of your pool beyond summer — swim all winter long without the expense or permanent eyesore of a built structure.

How the install works

Measured, anchored, and dialed in for your pool.

1

Measure the pool & deck

We review your pool shape, surrounding concrete, access points, and blower location so the dome is sized for your backyard.

2

Install concrete anchors

The vinyl perimeter is fastened to anchors set into the pool deck, creating a secure base for the air-supported enclosure.

3

Inflate, seal & service

We inflate the dome, check the seal, and tune the air pressure. Each year we can take it down and store it for summer — your pool and deck go right back to open-air, exactly as they were — then put it back up when the weather turns.
